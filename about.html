About Us

Who We Are


At Robinson Farms we are a first generation family-owned cattle farm that specializes in mixed angus cattle. The farm resides on the outskirts of Casa, Arkansas and serves as the home to approximately 300 cattle.

Who We Serve


At Robinson Farms we offer our high-quality beef cattle to beef producers and other farmers with either large or small farms. By raising cattle in a natural and healthy free-range environment, we also seek to serve our consumers by offering the highest quality cattle we can provide.

Our Business Model


The business model for Robinson Farms is based around the raising of high-quality beef cattle, especially our mixed angus. We have historically operated largely through cattle barns as an intermediary seller, but we have begun expanding our sales to include online sales in 2020. We operate primarily in a business to business model(B2B), but we are also eager to conduct business in a business to consumer(B2C) format when inquired. Butchering is not a service that we provide.
